Wedding

Thank you for downloading the Wedding Connect mobile application. You will now be able to upload your photos directly to a photo album. In order to do that you will first need to provide an album code. Your event hosts will be able to provide you with this code. Once you enter this good you are able to start uploading photos by clicking the Upload Photo link.

Each time you open this application we check if you have an album code saved. If you do we authenticate it and if it authenticates you are taken to the page to upload photos. If it does not you are provided with a link to where you are able to enter a new album code. Album codes do not last forever. They are only good from 2 hours before until 12 hours after the start of a particular event.
